Tank Serpe-Brown

The cutest cat that ever lived

Here's a timeline of Tank's life:

  • 2011 - Born in Tucson, Arizona
  • 2016 - Adopted from PACC in Tucson by Joey Brown and Michael Serpe. Immediately beaten into submission by older adopted brother-cat, Ottomarie
  • 2017 - Becomes an indoor-outdoor cat
  • 2018 - Secedes the local powers and becomes High King of Jacobus Ave
  • 2019 - Leaves Downtown and becomes a Midtown indoor prince
  • 2020 - Loses beloved and much feared older brother-cat, Ottomarie, to an early morning sneezing fit which stole his soul
  • 2021 - Moves to new apartment and sinks into a life of cushy middle-age at the ripe age of ten
  • ... and beyond - Life looks good for Tank. He has plans to move to The Land with Michael once the quonset hut is fully built and livable. He is particularly looking forward to meeting his future dog-sister, and growing old on a hassock in the Arizona room, watching the hummingbirds drink from the squash flowers and orange blossoms in the shadow of A Mountain

"Tank is a stinky, sweet, protective and incredibly loud creature. His early-morning operatic arias are the stuff of local legend."

- J. Brown
